About Naptick Sleep LLM

Founded by sleep science and AI experts, we're building the world's first domain-specific language model to transform sleep health through research-driven innovation.

Our Mission

To revolutionize sleep science and healthcare by making advanced AI accessible to researchers, clinicians, and developers worldwide.

Every night, billions of people struggle with sleep issues in a $500B global industry. Traditional approaches to sleep analysis are time-intensive, subjective, and limited in scope. We're changing this with the first AI model specifically trained for sleep science applications.

Our Story

2022

Founded at Stanford

Jennifer and Raj met at Stanford's AI4ALL lab, recognizing the need for domain-specific AI in sleep medicine. Initial research funded by Stanford HAI.

2023

Seed Funding & Team Building

Raised $3M seed round led by General Catalyst. Recruited world-class team from Stanford, MIT, and leading tech companies.

2024

First Model & Validation

Launched Sleep LLM v1 with 94.2% accuracy. Published groundbreaking research in Nature Digital Medicine. Began clinical partnerships.

2025

Series A & Scale

Raised $25M Series A led by Andreessen Horowitz. Expanding platform capabilities and building enterprise partnerships globally.
